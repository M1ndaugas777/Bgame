Bgame is a fantazy rpg game, where you start with nothing but a rusty sword, some bread, and a few pieces of gold in your pocked. Even thought your beggining are humble, the wise village elder had a prophecy that you are destined to become a most powerfull warrior of all time and defeat The Demon Lord B! After hearing this from the village elder, you pick up your rusty sword and begin a quest to reach The Demon Lord's B palace and defeat him! Best of luck adventurer!

  • While on your quest to fullfill your destiny, you will find:

  • Over 50 different enemies to defeat.

  • 7 different locations on your way to Demon Lord's Palace.

  • You will be able to aquire additional knowledge from the village elder.

  • You will be able to pray to ancient totems and aquire additional strength.

  • Nearly 100 different items to purchase.
